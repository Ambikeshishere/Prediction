ADMISSION PREDICTOR — Project Description
==========================================

WHAT IS THIS PROJECT?
---------------------
Admission Predictor is a web-based analytics dashboard that takes raw admission
data (date, region, center, number of admissions) and transforms it into a
complete business intelligence report. It shows weekly trends, forecasts monthly
totals, identifies underperforming weeks, compares centers against each other,
and generates a full business analysis report — all in the browser with no
server or database required.

It is designed for education business analysts, operations teams, and
decision-makers who need quick, clear answers from their admission data
without relying on data engineers or expensive BI tools.


HOW DOES IT WORK?
------------------
1. DATA LOADING
   The app fetches admission data directly from a published Google Sheets CSV
   link every time the page loads. This means the data is always live — when
   someone updates the Google Sheet, the dashboard reflects the new numbers on
   the next page refresh. No manual file uploads needed for day-to-day use.

2. DATA PROCESSING
   Raw CSV rows are parsed into structured records. Each record gets:
   - A cycle year (Sep-Aug academic cycle)
   - A cycle week number (Week 1 = first admission date of that center,
     so every center's timeline starts independently)

3. ANALYSIS ENGINE
   The app runs multiple analyses in sequence:
   
   a) Monthly Aggregation — Groups admissions by calendar month to compute
      totals, averages, medians, and month-over-month growth rates.
   
   b) Weekly Timeline — Groups admissions by cycle week across all selected
      centers. Computes week-over-week change and a rolling 4-week average
      to smooth out noise.
   
   c) Center-wise Analysis — Breaks down each center's admissions by week.
      Identifies which weeks a center underperforms or overperforms compared
      to the overall average across all centers.
   
   d) Similarity Matching — Converts each center's weekly pattern into a
      percentage vector (what share of total admissions happened in each
      week). Then computes Euclidean distance between all center pairs to
      find which centers behave most similarly.
   
   e) Boost Detection — Flags weeks where a center's admission share is
      more than 10% below the overall average. Quantifies the gap and
      suggests how many additional admissions are needed to recover.
   
   f) Month Projection — For the current calendar month, calculates a daily
      run rate from data collected so far and projects the month-end total.
      Also breaks it down week-by-week within the month.
   
   g) Ensemble Prediction — Uses four statistical models to forecast future
      months:
      - Linear Regression (25% weight)
      - Weighted Moving Average (30% weight)
      - Exponential Smoothing (30% weight)
      - Seasonal Decomposition (15% weight)
      The combined ensemble is more robust than any single model.

4. REPORT GENERATION
   All analyses are compiled into a structured business report with:
   - Executive Summary (key metrics at a glance)
   - Weekly Timeline (every week with date range)
   - Center Analysis (per-center weekly tables)
   - Boost Strategy (visual comparison cards)
   - Center Comparison (big-number summary cards)
   - Forecast (current month projection with progress bar)
   - Risk Assessment (trend + volatility score)

5. INTERACTIVE TABS
   Below the report, six tabs provide deeper drill-down:
   - Weekly Breakdown (full table with bar charts)
   - Month Projection (current month week-by-week)
   - Boost Strategy (center-wise weak weeks)
   - Center Comparison (ranked table with similarity scores)
   - Forecast (prediction table with confidence intervals)
   - Raw Data (searchable, sortable record-level table)


WHAT CAN THIS PROJECT DO?
--------------------------
Immediate capabilities:
  - Automatically load live admission data from Google Sheets
  - Show total admissions, monthly average, median, and trend at a glance
  - Display every cycle week's admissions with date ranges and growth rates
  - Project how many admissions the current month will end with
  - Compare the latest week against the previous week visually
  - Count how many centers follow similar admission patterns
  - Identify which weeks are underperforming across all centers
  - Forecast the next month's admissions using four statistical models
  - Generate a printable business analysis report
  - Work in both light and dark themes
  - Filter data by region and center using multi-select dropdowns
  - Load on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile

Business value:
  - Operations teams can spot weak weeks early and plan marketing pushes
  - Center managers can see how their center compares to similar centers
  - Leadership can get a quick month-end forecast without waiting for
    manual reports
  - The live Google Sheets connection means the data is always current
    without any deployment or backend changes
  - The visual cards and plain-English summaries make the report
    accessible to anyone regardless of technical background


WHY WAS THIS PROJECT CREATED?
------------------------------
This project was created to solve a specific business problem: understanding
admission patterns across multiple education centers and predicting future
admissions without relying on complex BI tools or data pipelines.

The core need was:
  1. A simple, visual way to see what is happening RIGHT NOW with admissions
  2. A forecast of where the current month will end up
  3. A comparison of which centers are performing similarly so patterns
     can be studied and replicated
  4. Early warning signals when a week or center is falling behind

Traditional approaches (Excel sheets, manual reports, data analyst requests)
were too slow for daily decision-making. This tool was built to give
instant, actionable answers the moment admission data is available.

The design philosophy was simplicity:
  - No backend server to maintain
  - No database to manage
  - No build step or compilation
  - Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that runs anywhere
  - Google Sheets as the single source of truth (easy for non-technical
    team members to update)
  - Visual cards and progress bars instead of dense tables
  - Plain English summaries instead of statistical jargon
  - The report should be understandable by anyone at a glance


WHO IS THIS FOR?
-----------------
  - Business Analysts who need quick answers from admission data
  - Operations Managers who track weekly admission performance
  - Center Heads who want to see how their center compares
  - Leadership Teams who need monthly forecasts for planning
  - Anyone who works with education admission data and wants
    a clear, visual dashboard without technical overhead


WHAT MAKES THIS DIFFERENT?
----------------------------
  - Zero setup — just open the URL and data loads automatically
  - Live data — connected to Google Sheets, always up to date
  - No backend — runs entirely in the browser
  - Visual-first — big numbers, progress bars, color coding
  - Self-explanatory — designed so anyone can understand at a glance
  - Per-center week numbering — Week 1 starts from each center's first
    admission, not from a fixed calendar date, giving a more accurate
    picture of each center's journey
